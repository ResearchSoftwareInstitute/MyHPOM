How it works

1

Create data

Collect your data using the same methods you use now. xDCIShare supports a broad set of scientific data types.
2

Upload to xDCIShare

Upload your data files to xDCIShare through the web user interface. xDCIShare will automatically extract as much metadata as it can from the files you upload.
3

Describe with metadata

Use xDCIShare's simple metadata entry forms to finish describing your data so that your colleagues can find, access, and interpret it.
4

Share with colleagues

You choose who has access to the data and models you have uploaded to xDCIShare. You can share with individual users or publish your resources for everyone to access.

What you can do with xDCIShare

Share your data and models with colleagues
Manage who has access to the content that you share
Share, access, visualize and manipulate a broad set of scientific data types and models
Use the web services API to program automated and client access
Publish data and models to meet the requirements of your data management plan
Discover and access data and models published by others
Use web apps to visualize, analyze and run models on data in xDCIShare
