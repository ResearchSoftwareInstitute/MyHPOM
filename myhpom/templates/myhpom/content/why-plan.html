Why Plan?

A quick guide to how an advance care plan keeps your care in check.

{% scribble 'why-plan' 'overview' %} An advance directive is a legal document which allows you to record your healthcare wishes in case you can no longer speak for yourself. These documents can also be combined with Power of Attorney documents and are used by medical professionals as well as family and caregivers to ensure your decisions are heard and respected. {% endscribble %}
Ownership
It empowers you to make your own healthcare decisions.
Communication
It allows you a clear, concise way to share your choices.
Responsibility
Reduces difficulty on yourself and those you care about.
Peace of mind
Brings confidence that your wishes will be heard.
